# Pets

This category includes real-world problems related to pet care, pet health, caregiver routines, veterinary follow-up, and pet-related records.

These problems often affect pet owners, caregivers, veterinarians, clinics, and pet service providers. They usually involve fragmented information, preventive care, routine follow-up, and difficulty keeping a clear view of the animal’s history and well-being.

Typical topics in this category include:

- pet history and medical records
- basic health monitoring
- preventive care routines
- caregiver coordination
- veterinary follow-up
- day-to-day pet care visibility

This category is a good fit for problems where people care about the animal, but the information, routine, or health follow-up is too fragmented or reactive.

## Examples
- Disorganized pet history / lack of a centralized pet record
- Difficulty monitoring basic pet health
