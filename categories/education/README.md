# Education

This category includes real-world problems related to learning, teaching, student support, school communication, and educational follow-up.

These problems may affect students, teachers, parents, coordinators, or institutions. They usually involve visibility, personalization, learning progress, communication gaps, or operational difficulties in educational environments.

Typical topics in this category include:

- student progress tracking
- parent visibility into learning
- classroom and school communication
- personalized teaching challenges
- learning difficulties and interventions
- academic follow-up and support

This category is a good fit for problems where education is happening, but important signals about learning, progress, or support are not clear enough.

## Examples
- Parents lack clear visibility into the student’s real progress
- Lack of personalized teaching based on the student’s actual difficulties
