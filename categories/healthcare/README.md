# Healthcare

This category includes real-world problems related to patient care, clinics, treatment follow-up, scheduling, communication, and healthcare operations.

These problems may affect patients, caregivers, healthcare professionals, administrative staff, or clinics. They often involve continuity of care, visibility between appointments, operational coordination, and practical barriers in care delivery.

Typical topics in this category include:

- patient follow-up outside the clinic
- appointment and exam scheduling
- care continuity
- treatment adherence
- symptom tracking
- communication between patient and provider

This category is a good fit for problems where care exists, but visibility, coordination, or follow-up breaks down in everyday practice.

## Examples
- Difficulty tracking the patient’s progress outside the clinic
- Confusing scheduling of appointments and exams
