# Logistics & Transport

This category includes real-world problems related to delivery operations, transportation workflows, route performance, vehicle usage, and logistics visibility.

These problems may affect logistics teams, dispatchers, drivers, managers, customers, and support teams. They often involve delays, weak operational visibility, fragmented updates, route inefficiency, and reactive decision-making.

Typical topics in this category include:

- delivery delays
- route performance visibility
- driver and vehicle performance
- shipment exceptions
- operational bottlenecks
- transport coordination and tracking

This category is a good fit for problems where the logistics operation runs, but teams struggle to understand delays, react quickly, or improve performance with confidence.

## Examples
- Delivery delays
- Little visibility into performance by route, vehicle, or driver
