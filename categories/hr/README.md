# HR

This category includes real-world problems related to hiring, onboarding, retention, people operations, and employee experience.

These problems may affect recruiters, HR teams, managers, new hires, and employees. They often involve screening, process overload, poor follow-up, weak onboarding, and lack of visibility into people-related workflows.

Typical topics in this category include:

- candidate screening
- recruitment workflow
- onboarding and early adaptation
- retention in the first months
- hiring quality
- employee follow-up and people operations

This category is a good fit for problems where the company has a people process, but that process is slow, fragmented, hard to trust, or difficult to sustain with quality.

## Examples
- Manual candidate screening, but low accuracy when automated
- Low retention due to failures in the first few months or lack of follow-up
