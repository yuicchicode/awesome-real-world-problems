# International Trade

This category includes real-world problems related to import and export operations, customs, logistics coordination, shipment visibility, documentation, and decision-making across multiple trade stakeholders.

These problems often affect importers, exporters, customs brokers, suppliers, carriers, freight agents, and internal operations teams. They usually involve fragmented communication, missing visibility, slow decisions, and process dependency across many actors.

Typical topics in this category include:

- communication between trade stakeholders
- shipment and documentation visibility
- customs-related coordination
- milestone tracking across import/export flows
- decision-making with fragmented information
- operational dependency on scattered updates

This category is a good fit for problems where a foreign trade process exists, but people cannot see, coordinate, or act on the full picture clearly enough.

## Examples
- Poor communication between importer, customs broker, supplier, and carrier
- Slow decision-making due to lack of centralized information
