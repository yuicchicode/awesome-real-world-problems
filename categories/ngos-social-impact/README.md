# NGOs & Social Impact

This category includes real-world problems related to NGOs, community projects, social initiatives, volunteer coordination, and impact operations.

These problems often affect nonprofit teams, coordinators, volunteers, donors, and communities being served. They usually involve limited resources, informal workflows, communication gaps, and difficulty turning goodwill into sustainable operations.

Typical topics in this category include:

- fundraising and donation challenges
- volunteer coordination
- social project operations
- supporter engagement
- community-driven workflows
- limited-resource management

This category is a good fit for problems where the mission is meaningful and the need is real, but the structure, funding, or coordination is not strong enough to support the work consistently.

## Examples
- Difficulty raising donations
- Poorly organized volunteers
