# Daily Life

This category includes real-world problems related to everyday personal, family, and home routines.

These problems are usually not about large institutions or formal business operations. Instead, they come from daily coordination, mental load, recurring responsibilities, and the small systems people create to keep life working.

Typical topics in this category include:

- household task organization
- family coordination
- child routine management
- daily planning and reminders
- shared responsibilities at home
- recurring personal or family logistics

This category is a good fit for problems where people rely too much on memory, informal communication, or constant follow-up to keep daily life running.

## Examples
- Organization of household tasks
- Child routine management
