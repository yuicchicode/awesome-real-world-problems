# Sales & Marketing

This category includes real-world problems related to lead generation, funnel visibility, campaign attribution, sales-marketing alignment, and commercial follow-up.

These problems often affect marketing teams, sales teams, growth teams, and operations leaders. They usually involve fragmented lead data, conflicting reports, weak funnel visibility, and misalignment between acquisition and revenue workflows.

Typical topics in this category include:

- lead capture and attribution
- fragmented lead information
- sales and marketing alignment
- funnel reporting
- opportunity tracking
- shared visibility across commercial teams

This category is a good fit for problems where leads, campaigns, and opportunities exist, but the data, definitions, or operational flow are too fragmented to support clear action.

## Examples
- Lead acquisition information scattered across multiple different platforms
- Sales and marketing do not see the same lead and opportunity numbers
