# Lead acquisition information scattered across multiple different platforms

## Problem summary
Companies often generate leads from many sources, but the information remains fragmented across forms, ad platforms, messaging apps, CRMs, spreadsheets, and manual processes. This weakens visibility, slows response, and creates duplicated or incomplete lead histories.

## Real-world report
I know the company is generating leads, but I cannot see the full picture in one place. Some leads come from the website, some from landing pages, some from Meta Ads, some from Google Ads, some through WhatsApp, some from events, some from email automation, and some are entered manually in spreadsheets or messages.

That means marketing sees one number in one platform, sales sees another number somewhere else, and someone else is trying to reconcile everything manually. Sometimes the same lead appears twice with different information. Sometimes the lead arrives without context, so no one knows which campaign brought it in, what content it interacted with, or who should take ownership next.

The frustrating part is that the lead journey becomes almost invisible. We know a person entered the funnel, but we cannot easily see where they came from, what happened next, and whether they actually became an opportunity.

## Why this is difficult
The real challenge is not only collecting leads. It is normalizing, deduplicating, contextualizing, and routing them while preserving source attribution and journey history.

## Why it matters
Scattered lead data creates rework, weakens decision-making, delays sales response, and makes it harder to connect marketing effort to actual business outcomes.

## Project opportunity
This is an excellent project problem because it touches ingestion pipelines, data reconciliation, attribution, workflow routing, dashboards, and integrations across real business tools.

## Technical requirements
- Ingest leads from multiple sources
- Normalize fields from inconsistent inputs
- Deduplicate lead records
- Preserve source, campaign, and attribution context
- Track status changes across the funnel
- Support ownership and assignment logic
- Keep a timeline of interactions and source events
- Support marketing and sales views with shared data foundations
- Provide filters by source, campaign, stage, and date
- Allow integrations with forms, ads, CRM, and messaging channels

## Suggested MVP
- Unified lead inbox
- Source attribution fields
- Deduplication logic
- Lead status pipeline
- Basic dashboard by source and stage
- Ownership assignment

## Possible extensions
- Lead scoring
- Automatic routing rules
- Campaign performance reconciliation
- CRM sync
- AI-generated lead summaries
